    Our Services

    • Our mission is to minimize the risk of recruiting a new employee – a costly and sensitive process. In order to do so we use valid assessment tools starting at the recruiting stage and on.
      The employee recruitment stage is followed by effective onboarding, training and optimized manager – employee match.
      Development of diagnostic tools throughout employee lifecycle in addition to those commonly used.
      Employee retention practices in order to minimize employee turnover       Employee Recruitment
    • Key employees are the ones who will transform your business into what it could be, a methodological approach will increase the probability of their staying productive and engaged.
      Defining criteria and mapping talents throughout the organization (mostly relevant for large organizations)
      Building a developmental career path for organizational talents, among other retention strategies.
      Succession planning practices for years to come.       Talent Management
    • Strategic decisions are usually followed by an implementation plan, sometimes by an organizational transformation. The execution plan is usually supported by various tools.
      Building a strategic implementation roadmap including effective work processes, management routines and KPIs.
      Organizational structure optimization based on job analyses combined with new business goals that have been set.
      Corporate culture monitoring and interventions for cultural transformation, along with ongoing process consulting for managers across the organization.       Strategy Execution
    • We believe refining a business question, challenging conventional thinking and conducting extensive research accordingly will lead to a focused, innovative and yet feasible solution.
      Detecting market opportunities and limitations by using methodological analysis of market size, growth rate, market trends and other qualitative and quantitative measurements.
      Competition analysis which provides a better understanding of market landscape, giving an insightful benchmark.
      Building a business case for strategic change if needed and focus on strategic planning.       Strategic Consulting

    About Us

    We are a boutique consulting firm providing Strategic and Organizational Consulting Services to companies, concentrating on bridging the gap between the business growth opportunities to be pursued and the actual organizational capability to do so.
